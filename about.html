I am the owner of The Cakery. I'm creative and talented pastry chef who has a passion for baking and decorating cakes that bring a smile to people's faces. I opened this shop to share our love of baking with the community, offering both custom and traditional cakes and baked goods.

The Cakery starts off small, offering a limited selection of cakes and baked goods, but quickly gains a reputation for its high-quality ingredients and unique flavors.

We introduces its first line of custom cakes, offering customers the ability to create their own unique and personalized desserts for special occasions like birthdays and weddings.

We opens its first satellite
location, expanding its reach and making it easier for customers to enjoy its delicious cakes.

As we approaches its 12th anniversary, it looks to the future, exploring new flavors and techniques to continue pushing the boundaries of what a cake can be.
